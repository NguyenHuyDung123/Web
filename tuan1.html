One particular book which ia recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Gwrisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and reakizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well writen and interesting.Other books by John Gwrisham incluse The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client
